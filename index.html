The Lost But Never Found

Rated M just to be on the safe side.

'Loneliness is to be without, and to be without is loneliness. No one truly understands a person. They are not them, so they will never understand. Words are only words without proof or action. Belief is trust in the unknown or uncertain. Trust is meaningless. Worthless. Trust is nothing but a lie.'

Ravyn, a rather smart and delicate child has grown up to be empty. To have no one to run to and care for her since a young age. At the age of four her parents tried to kill her she ran off into the forest. Living with the animals and nature. Learning their ways. Two years after she entered that forest she was found. Armed men tried to take her, but she didn't go without putting up a fight. The king of the land has taken interest in her and makes her a bodyguard for both him and his heir: Luka.

'To be human is to be confused. They are born into this world confuse and lost. Unsure of their role in this game. What is life? What is death? What does it mean to live? The lost could not be found, because the ones here are always lost.'

Ten years have passed and now a well trained assassin/bodyguard, Ravyn is tormented by the king and Luka for their own entertainment. What will become of the two, should anything happen that may change their lives? Will a romance bloom or will Ravyn continue to be empty?
